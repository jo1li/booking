Biography

The Los Angeles-based, indie outfit Run River North release sophomore album Drinking From A Salt Pond led by lead single “Run Or Hide.” The album lands at #6 on the Billboard Heatseekers’ chart and #3 on the Alternative New Artist Albums’ chart. Working with producer Lars Stalfors (Cold War Kids, Deap Vally, HEALTH, Matt and Kim), the band leaves behind its familiar folksy roots to craft an ambitious rock album.
